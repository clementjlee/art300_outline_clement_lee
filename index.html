    • What is the definition of Architecture?
      • Architecture is the built realization of a particular concept, or idea.
      • The profession of designing buildings, open areas, communities and other artificial constructions and environments, usually within some regard to aesthetic effect. Architecture often includes design or selection of furnishings and decorations, supervision of construction work, and the examination, restoration, and remodeling of existing buildings.
      • 1st century BC roman architect Virtruvius explain - according to his book De Architectura. Architecture consists of three things:
        • Firmness
          • Structural integrity and durability.
        • Commodity
          • Spatial functionality, or “serving its purpose”, and fulfilling functionality for which the building is constructed.
        • Delight
          • The building is not only aesthetically and visually pleasing, but also lifts the spirits and stimulates senses.
      • Architecture is an expression of the societal values, desires, ways of thinking, and ideals.
